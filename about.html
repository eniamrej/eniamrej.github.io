About us

Origin

5 Star Tax Solutions Inc. has been serving clients since its founding in 2008. It was founded by Laquita Alexander. Laquita started this business to help add a more personal touch to preparing taxes. She wanted to help those in need of a quick, affortable, and safe tax service. In the beginning she would personally visit the homes of the clients, discuss their tax situations, and get any tax information needed. She would also drop off their taxes once it was done. With the improvement in technology she has made changes and made it even easier for her clients when preparing their taxes.

Our Commtment to You

Here at 5 Star Tax Solutions we care deeply about our clients. Our mission is to maximize your tax experience and make the process as easy as possible. We are very transparent with our process and keep the client as well informed as possible. We take into account our clients specific needs and challenges and tailor our apporach to fit your specific situation. We will take on any client who is in need of our help.
